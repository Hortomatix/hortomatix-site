// company

Built by people who've done the work.

Robert Clark founder & director

Hortomatix was founded by Robert Clark, a landscape gardener with direct operational experience across every layer of the green industry: institutional grounds maintenance, wholesale supply, retail trade, and running a landscaping business.

We build software the industry actually wants to use, because we've done the work it's designed to support.

That domain knowledge informs every product decision: features that solve genuine pain points, vendor-neutral integrations, honest commercial terms, and platform neutrality as a long-term commitment.
